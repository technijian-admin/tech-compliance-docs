AFFG-001 — Incident Response Plan
1. Purpose and Scope

This Incident Response Plan (IRP) establishes written procedures for American Fundstars Financial Group LLC (AFFG) to detect, respond to, contain, investigate, and recover from security incidents involving unauthorized access to — or reasonably likely unauthorized access to — customer information, as required by SEC Regulation S-P (17 CFR §248.30).

This plan applies to all AFFG systems, personnel, and service providers that store, process, or transmit customer information, including: Microsoft 365, client account management systems, trade execution platforms, local and cloud backup systems (Synology NAS), and network infrastructure (Sophos Firewall AFFG-HQ-FW-02). This plan integrates with AFFG's Business Continuity Plan for operational recovery procedures.

5. Containment Protocols

5.1 Immediate Containment (within 1 hour for P1)

  1. Isolate compromised systems — Disconnect via Huntress remote isolation or manual disconnection. Do NOT power off (preserves volatile memory).
  2. Revoke compromised credentials — Force password reset on all suspected accounts via M365 Admin Center. Revoke all active sessions.
  3. Block malicious indicators — Add IPs, domains, and file hashes to Sophos and Cisco Umbrella block lists.
  4. Preserve network traffic logs — Export Sophos Firewall logs for the relevant period before log rotation.
  5. Notify IRT — Ravi Jain convenes the IRT within 30 minutes of P1 determination.
